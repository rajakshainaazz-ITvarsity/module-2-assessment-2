Coricraft

Quality Furniture and Homeware.
Coricraft supply stylish, comfortable furniture for living rooms, dining rooms and bedrooms.

We're passionate about selling high-quality, affordable furniture, and our impressive range is available in both modern and classic styles. We're known for our range of wooden furniture and customisable couches. We design and craft our home furniture with the goal of creating pieces that you'll love having in your house.

Coricraft have almost 50 furniture stores across Southern Africa. We have stores in South Africa, Namibia and Botswana, and you can buy our furniture online.

As a homegrown South African company, we are dedicated to creating sustainable job opportunities and keeping local talent alive. Each and every made-to-order couch, headboard and dining chair is handcrafted and upholstered by our Cape Town-based team of artisans, thus giving our customers the opportunity to support local industry by purchasing beautifully crafted items, at a competitive price point.
