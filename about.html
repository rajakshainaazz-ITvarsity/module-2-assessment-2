ABOUT

Pine City Mall (PCM) is Pine City's largest indoor shopping mall and entertainment center, featuring a contemporary mix of stores that is both diverse and family focused - with specialty stores such as Pandora and Rip Curl.

While shopping at PCM, be sure to check out the food court, and make your trip complete by watching a family movie or riding the indoor carousel.

This conveniently located mall features favourite stores such as Legit, iStore, Pick 'n Pay and many more. Shoppers are offered the ease of storefront and undercover parking and may choose to stroll sidewalks and enjoy the thrills of all our outdoor entertainment choices.
