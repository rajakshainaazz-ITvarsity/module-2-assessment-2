Ocean Basket

Ocean Basket is South Africa's favourite seafood restaurant, committed to giving you delicious seafood and that 'home-away- from-home' feeling to remind you of what it's like to sit at the family table and share a meal with your nearest and dearest. We offer an array of Mediterranean inspired dishes from prawns, sushi, and calamari to our famous fish and chips and, of course, our legendary creamy lemon sauce. Visit any of our restaurants to share one of our bountiful platters, dip your prawn into our trio of sauces or dig into our complimentary bread.

The first Ocean Basket seafood restaurant opened its doors in 1995 in Menlyn Park, Pretoria. Fats Lazarides had seen the opportunity to create a restaurant that served delicious seafood at affordable prices. In those days, seafood restaurants were reserved for the upper crust, and a portion of prawns was a real extravagance. Fortunately, Fats had always been one to swim against the current.
